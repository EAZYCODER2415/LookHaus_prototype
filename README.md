# LookHaus_prototype
Prototype room-furnish app

**Introduction**
As urban infrastructure gets increasingly compact (especially Bangkok and soon will be Chiang Mai), digital nomads, independent workers, and families are eliminating the excess fees of having to buy a full-set house, prioritizing flexibility and accessibility to every-day locations, thus SELECTING condominiums as their turfs. 
Although there are an abundance of condo agencies, today modernists value authenticity and a comfortable resident experience. Other than external amenity zones, agencies should EMPLOY an AI agent that can help FURNISH a room according to the person’s preferences, besides providing static designs, in respect to the condo’s provided room measurements for smooth precision.

**Idea Summary**
Okay so my AI app idea is a room-furnishing app that generates images of room layouts and curates selections of furniture options, in respect to the condominium's registered room measurements to prevent AI from making up information that doesn't exist or does not correspond to the company's layouts. The company can register their room measurements and the person buying the rooms can allow for more personalization, eliminating non-niche rooms in many condominium projects.
